Pole Dance

For Artistic Athletes and Aerialists Everywhere

Modern pole dance is performed on steel, brass or chrome poles, spinning or static. Poles were incorporated into burlesque dancing as a prop in the 1920’s. By the 1980’s dancers began to develop athletic tricks such as climbs, spins, and inversions, giving birth to a global industry and the badass art form that we know today.
